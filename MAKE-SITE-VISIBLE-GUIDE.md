## Step 1: Submit to Google Search Console (TODAY)

**Time: 10 minutes**

1. Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Click "Start now"
3. Login with your Google account
4. Click "Add property"
5. Choose "URL prefix"
6. Enter: `https://deft-licorice-b8a79a.netlify.app`
7. Verify ownership:
   - **Easiest method:** HTML file upload
   - Download the HTML file Google gives you
   - Upload it to your GitHub repo
   - Push to GitHub (Netlify auto-deploys)
   - Click "Verify" in Google Search Console

8. Once verified, submit your sitemap:
   - Click "Sitemaps" in left menu
   - Enter: `sitemap.xml`
   - Click "Submit"

9. Request indexing:
   - Go to "URL Inspection"
   - Enter your homepage URL
   - Click "Request Indexing"

**Result:** Google will crawl your site within 24-48 hours
